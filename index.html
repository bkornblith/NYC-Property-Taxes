NYC Property Tax Lookup

Live DOF Data

Queries the NYC Department of Finance directly from your browser — no proxy, no restrictions.

How this works

  1. Geocodes your address via the NYC Planning GeoSearch API to get the Borough-Block-Lot (BBL)
  2. Queries the NYC DOF Property Tax portal using your BBL to retrieve live assessment data
  3. Fetches your tax bills directly from the DOF document system (EDMS) for exact annual amounts
